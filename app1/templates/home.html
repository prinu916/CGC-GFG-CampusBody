Where Students Become Engineers

The GeeksForGeeks CGC Chapter is the official student-run tech community at CGC University. We run workshops, hackathons, coding contests, and mentorship — all built around one idea: real learning through real building.

Whether you're in your first semester or already shipping projects, there's a place for you here.
